About Us

Rano Da Dhaaba first opened its doors in 1999 by Mrs Daljit's parents. Mrs Daljit took over the restaurant in 2007 and has successfully run the business since. Along with being the owner, Mrs Daljit is the headchef as well. No one knows the taste of authentic desi food better than a passionate chef born and raised in Punjab.


Our Mission

Rano Da Dhaaba serves to bring a new experience of desi, punjabi cuisine to Kenya. Our food is fresh and homemade because we prioritise quality so you can enjoy and savour every bite.


A few stand outs:

Our speciality, SAAG AND MAKKI DI ROTI, is flat bread made with maize flour accompained with cooked mustard green leaves garnished with butter, ginger and red chillies. A very nutritional, healthy and most importantly delicious meal, commonly enjoyed during the winter.

Kheer is a very popular desert in India. It is a pudding made by boiling milk, rice and sugar or jaggery. Garnished with almonds and pistachios.
